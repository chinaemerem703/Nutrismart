Weight Management Tips

Sustainable approaches to healthy weight

Eat Mindfully

Eat slowly, without distractions. Pay attention to hunger and fullness cues to prevent overeating.

Behavior

Increase Protein Intake

Protein increases satiety and preserves muscle mass during weight loss. Include lean protein at every meal.

Macronutrients

Fill Half Your Plate with Vegetables

Vegetables are low in calories but high in nutrients and fiber, helping you feel full while managing calories.

Meal Composition

Plan Your Meals

Meal planning helps you make healthier choices and avoid impulsive eating. Prepare meals in advance when possible.

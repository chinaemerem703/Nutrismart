Expert Nutrition Guidance

Evidence-based tips to support your health journey

Choose Low GI Foods

Focus on foods with a low glycemic index like quinoa, sweet potatoes, and non-starchy vegetables to maintain stable blood sugar levels.

Blood Sugar Management

Monitor Carbohydrate Intake

Count carbs and distribute them evenly throughout the day. Aim for 45-60g per meal depending on your plan.

Meal Planning

Include Protein with Each Meal

Protein slows carbohydrate absorption and helps prevent blood sugar spikes. Good sources include lean meats, fish, eggs, and legumes.

Nutrition Balance

Limit Added Sugars

Avoid sugary drinks, desserts, and processed foods. Read labels carefully and choose sugar-free alternatives when possible.

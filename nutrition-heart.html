Expert Nutrition Guidance

Evidence-based tips to support your health journey

Omega-3 Fatty Acids

Eat fatty fish like salmon, mackerel, or sardines at least twice a week. Omega-3s reduce inflammation and support heart health.

Healthy Fats

Reduce Sodium Intake

Limit sodium to less than 2,300mg per day. Use herbs and spices for flavor instead of salt.

Blood Pressure

Increase Potassium

Eat potassium-rich foods like bananas, spinach, and sweet potatoes. Potassium helps counteract sodium's effects on blood pressure.

Minerals

Choose Healthy Fats

Replace saturated fats with unsaturated fats from olive oil, avocados, and nuts. Limit trans fats completely.
